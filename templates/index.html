About NITP Abuja Chapter

The Nigeria Institute of Town Planners (NITP) Abuja Chapter is the professional body for town planners in Nigeria's Federal Capital Territory. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable urban development and enhancing the quality of life in our cities through effective planning.

As a chapter, we work closely with government agencies, private sector organizations, and communities to ensure that development in Abuja follows best practices in urban planning and design.

Our Services

  • Professional Membership: We provide professional recognition and accreditation for qualified town planners.
  • Continuing Education: We offer opportunities for professional development and lifelong learning.
  • Site Analysis Reports (SAR): We issue official SAR certificates that are recognized by government agencies and development authorities.
  • Advocacy: We advocate for sustainable urban planning policies and practices.
